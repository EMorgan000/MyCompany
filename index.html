Welcome to Chico's!

Chico's has been the number 1 Mexican restaurant in the state of Missouri. After 25 years of service, and over a million customers served, we strive to continue providing the best tacos, burritos, and quesadillas for many years to come. Other than what we provide from our kitchens, we also belive in an excellent restaurant environment where friends and family can meet and make everlasting memories.

Our Story

Back in 1996, a group of talented cooks, including Chico, were fresh out of college and wanted to put their skills to the test. They decided to pool their remaining funds and talents and open a restaurant in the downtown Springfield area. They were able to afford a small brick building with a modest sized kitchen and got to work immediately; branching out to other Mexican restaurants for ideas and pinning up posters downtown to gain extra skills and recognition. After just a few years of hard work and perseverance, Chico's went from just another Mexican restaurant into the amazing place it is today.
